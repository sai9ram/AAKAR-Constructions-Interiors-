About AAKAR

Trusted Construction Experts in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana

AAKAR Constructions is a leading construction company specializing in residential and commercial projects. Headquartered in Hyderabad, we serve clients across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. We provide complete turnkey construction services—from planning and architectural design to execution and final delivery.

Our approach combines modern construction technologies with deep industry expertise to deliver durable, efficient, and high-quality structures. With a strong commitment to timelines and transparency, we ensure every project is delivered with precision and professionalism.
